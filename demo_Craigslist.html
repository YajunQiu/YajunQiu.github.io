Project Description

Craigslist is one of the most popular classified advertisement websites and it reaches out to more than 200 countries (Wikipedia). It features sections devoted to jobs, housing, personals, sale items, services, community happenings, and discussion forums. People use Craigslist to post ads and search for information. The target audiences of Craigslist are those who want to do business online. With a personal account, they can post advertisements in Craigslist. And they can purchase goods or share information there as well. Most Craigslist users have specific purposes. They know what they want some use Craigslist to search items that meet their specific needs.

Project Goal

The designing goal to is to improve the usability with minor change to the original design. Craigslist looks old-fashioned, its information is overwhelming and navigation system is inefficient. As a class assignment, the author will design the entrance (Main Page) and exit (Listing Page) of Craigslist, two most heavily used pages in Craigslist.

My Role & Duties

  • Redesign the Main Page based on the Nielson’s Heuristic on Usability.
