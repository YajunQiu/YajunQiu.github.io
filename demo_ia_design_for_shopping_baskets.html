Project Description

IA for shopping baskets is a PPT that records research on shopping carts icon (baskets) for online shopping websites. It is presented as a mid-term assignment for class Information Architecture and Design. In the presentation, the IA shopping cart standards and E-commerce standards are discussed with examples.

Project Goal

The primary goal is to provide a literature review of IA design of shopping carts in online shopping. The focus is on strategies to optimize user's online shopping experience.

My Role and Duties

  • Writer and speaker
  • Conducting literature review on online shopping carts IA standards.
  • Collecting and organize examples for IA standards
  • Presenting research results in public

Tools Used

PPT
