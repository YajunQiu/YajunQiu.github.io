Project Description

A small courseware made in 2020 to demonstrate video-based learning strategies in facilitating micro learning. Four round of quick-response posed for learner to answer and the total length of the video is 5 minutes. Different feedback are provided based on the decisions made by leaners themselves.

Project Goal

As a widely-accepted e-learning trend, VBL proved to increase learning retention. The mini-module is built to demonstrate the use as well as the benefit of VBL strategies in practice.
